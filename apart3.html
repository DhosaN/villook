Tropical Oasis Villa

Situated in Sanur, Tropical Oasis, Villa Mimba features accommodation with private pool, free WiFi and free private parking for guests who drive. The air-conditioned accommodation is 2.2 km from Sindhu Beach. Outdoor dining is also possible at the villa.

The spacious villa has 3 bedrooms, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a fully equipped kitchen with an oven and a microwave, a washing machine, and 3 bathrooms with a shower. The villa offers bed linen, towels and daily maid service.

Segara Beach is 2.6 km from the accommodation, while Udayana University is 6.3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 13 km from Tropical Oasis, Villa Mimba, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
